# Incident Response – Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Who do I notify if I spot suspicious activity?**  
A: Refer to the [Escalation Matrix](link-to-esc-matrix-page) for the appropriate contacts based on severity.

**Q: What is considered a security incident?**  
A: Any event that compromises—or could compromise—the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of information systems or data.

**Q: How quickly must a high-severity alert be triaged?**  
A: Within 30 minutes (see [SLA guidelines](link-to-sla-guidelines)).

**Q: What information should I include when reporting an incident?**  
A: Time and date, affected systems/users, description of suspicious activity, any immediate actions taken.

**Q: Where can I find the official IR procedures?**  
A: All procedures are stored in the [Procedures & Playbooks](link-to-procedures-page) section of the knowledge base.

**Q: Who can authorize communication to external parties?**  
A: Only the IR Lead or CISO, as per [Communication Policy](link-to-policy-page).
