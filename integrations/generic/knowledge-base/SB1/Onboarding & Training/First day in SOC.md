# First Day in the SOC – Analyst Checklist

- [ ] Received credentials for TheHive, Cortex, SIEM, and ticketing tools
- [ ] Read and signed Acceptable Use & Security Policy
- [ ] Met with SOC Lead for orientation
- [ ] Reviewed current major incidents and shift handover
- [ ] Added to all internal chat/channels
- [ ] Located escalation matrix and tool cheat sheets
